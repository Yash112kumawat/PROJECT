Why Yami Jewels ?

Authencity

Any product you purchase from Yami Jewels is crafted in India from pure 925 silver i.e. sterling silver. Yami Jewels is hallmarked by the Bureau of Indian Standards, so you can be sure that the products you purchase from us are authentic and pure. For your assurance, every piece comes with an authenticity certificate.

Rodhium Plated

Our products are coated with rhodium plating to lengthen the shine of the jewellery. This decelerates the tarnishing process and keeps your jewellery safe from scratches and adds an extra layer of shine.

Premium Packaging

All your orders will reach you in a premium Yami box that you can conveniently store your jewellery in. Your jewellery will arrive safely cushioned in the box. Inside the box, you will also find jewellery care instructions and an authenticity certificate.

Perfect Of Gifting

All Yami products come in a sturdy, premium box which makes it a great gifting option for your loved ones. As all rings are adjustable, you can gift Yami rings to anyone without worrying about their ring size.
